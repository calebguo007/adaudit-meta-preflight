# Policy Risk Playbook

The agent should catch policy and trust risks before any launch package is prepared.

High-risk claim patterns:
- Guaranteed outcomes: "guaranteed", "100% success", "land a job in 7 days", "lose 10 pounds in 30 days".
- Time-bound outcomes in sensitive domains.
- Before/after framing for health, body, finance, or employment when it implies guaranteed results.
- Personal attribute callouts: implying the viewer has a medical, financial, employment, or identity condition.
- Sensational or fear-heavy claims unsupported by evidence.
- Misleading scarcity or false urgency.

Sensitive domains:
- Employment and career outcomes.
- Credit, finance, investing, insurance, debt, lending.
- Health, wellness, weight loss, supplements, medical claims.
- Housing and real estate.
- Politics, social issues, and civic participation.

Repair strategy:
- Replace guaranteed outcomes with proof mechanisms.
- Replace personal attribute language with product capability language.
- Replace time-bound promises with diagnostic or educational framing.
- Add substantiation requirements to launch notes.
- Keep final execution paused until human review.
