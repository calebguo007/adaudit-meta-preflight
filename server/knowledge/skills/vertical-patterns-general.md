# Vertical Patterns General

AdAudit should not be tied to one vertical. It should infer the vertical and retrieve the relevant operating pattern.

Consumer apps:
- Test onboarding promise, social proof, habit loop, and friction removal.
- Avoid vague "AI-powered" claims. Show the user outcome or moment.

Short drama and entertainment:
- Test cliffhanger, relationship conflict, revenge, secret identity, status reversal, and episode CTA.
- Metrics often include CPI, trial start, first episode completion, ROAS, and retention.
- Strong creatives use story tension quickly; weak creatives over-explain the app.

E-commerce:
- Test product proof, offer clarity, comparison, UGC, review proof, objection handling.
- Watch margin, AOV, return rate, shipping claims, and discount dependency.

B2B SaaS:
- Test pain diagnosis, workflow automation, ROI proof, comparison, and role-specific language.
- Avoid broad persona targeting when ACV and sales motion require precision.

Lead generation:
- Test form friction, lead quality, qualification, and follow-up speed.
- A low CPL can still be bad if lead quality is weak.

Finance and health:
- Policy risk is high. Use educational and capability framing, not guaranteed outcomes.
